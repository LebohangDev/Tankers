Rules Of Game

  • Click the start button to start the round.
  • Game will then start, and enemy tanks will spawn randomly from any side of the screen.
  • Use the W and s to move up or down to move the tank around and the mouse cursor to aim/rotate the tank. It will aim there depending on where your cursor is; spacebar to shoot back!
  • To win the game, you must survive as long as possible without dying. You will have three lives; the game ends once all three lives are gone.
